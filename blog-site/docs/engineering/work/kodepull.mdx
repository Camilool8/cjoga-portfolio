# KODEPULL SRL

**Role:** Co-founder & CTO  
**Co-founder & CEO:** [Fernando Reyes](https://www.linkedin.com/in/fernando-reyes-b51662223/)  
**Founded:** 2025  
**Based:** Santo Domingo, DR  
**Site:** [kodepull.com](https://kodepull.com/en/)

KODEPULL SRL is the company Fernando Reyes and I founded in 2025. Where the other pages here cover work I've done for other people's organizations, this is the one I help own. I'm the CTO. Fernando is the CEO.

The public brand is **KodePull** and the marketing site lives at [kodepull.com](https://kodepull.com/en/). On this page I'll describe what we build, what I specifically own inside the company, and the projects that have moved through it so far.

## What we build

KodePull positions itself as enterprise-quality engineering for businesses that can't afford enterprise contracts. Six service areas: web development, custom software, infrastructure and cloud, mobile, AI and automation, and consulting and advisory. Twelve industries on the marketing site so far — the ones with the most pull right now are cloud, custom software, and senior consulting work, where the "enterprise-quality but not enterprise-contract" pitch lands hardest.

Our shape is small by design. Fernando and I lead, and we bring in specialist engineers, designers, and other partners based on what each project actually needs. We don't try to be a sixty-person agency.

## My role

As CTO I own the technical side: architecture, infrastructure design, the hands-on engineering on projects that need it, platform and vendor decisions, and the quality bar we ship under. Fernando owns client relationships, product strategy, and the work of making sure what we ship maps to real business outcomes. We're built to overlap on most decisions and disagree out loud when we should.

## Selected work

### UnicoWraps

An iPad app for an automotive retailer in the car-wrapping space. Customers in the store pick a wrap style on the iPad and see their own vehicle rendered in 3D in real time — color, texture, finish — instead of imagining from a printed catalog. The 3D showroom became the sales differentiator for the store. [Full case study](https://kodepull.com/en/work/unicowraps/) on kodepull.com.

### Tutorias Universitarias

A complete digital platform for a Dominican university-tutoring business at [tutoriasuniversitarias.com](https://tutoriasuniversitarias.com/). They came in WhatsApp-only: no website, no lead capture, no visibility into what marketing was actually working. We built the site (rankable on Google from day one), an admin panel, automated email follow-ups, and a real-time funnel dashboard. The business expanded its reach to students across multiple Spanish-speaking countries. [Full case study](https://kodepull.com/en/work/tutorias-universitarias/) on kodepull.com.

### Bank DevOps Portal

A self-service infrastructure portal for a banking institution's internal development organization, used daily by 500+ developers. [Vue.js](https://vuejs.org/) frontend, .NET API, integrations into [ArgoCD](https://argo-cd.readthedocs.io/), AKS, the Azure Portal, and private bank systems. Built-in templates and guardrails let teams self-provision repositories, manage permissions, surface deployment metrics, and request infrastructure through approved patterns instead of opening tickets. [Full case study](https://kodepull.com/en/work/portal-devops/) on kodepull.com.
